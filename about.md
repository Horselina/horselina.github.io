I write about Android internals, kernel and boot-chain security, and reverse engineering.

This site collects long-form technical notes from hands-on experiments — bootloader unlocking, firmware patching, debugging boot failures, and the occasional bricked device. The emphasis is on documenting the full path to a result, including dead ends, rather than presenting a tidy tutorial after the fact.
